How to use this data

This dashboard is meant to help you quickly understand where walkability is strongest, where it is weakest, and how that gap might evolve over time.

For policy & funding

  • Scan the distribution to see how many neighborhood areas sit in low vs. high walkability bands.
  • Use “States by Walkability” to identify states that consistently underperform their peers.
  • Start with the recommendations list when choosing where to target limited funds or pilot programs.

For planning & design

  • Compare current vs. forecast to understand whether gaps between states are expected to narrow or widen.
  • Drill into the State Spotlight by clicking a bar to see how a single state’s score and outlook compare.
  • Pair with local knowledge (corridor plans, zoning, transit projects) to translate low scores into specific projects.
